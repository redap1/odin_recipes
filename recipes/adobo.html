Chicken Adobo

Description

Chicken adobo is considered the national dish of the Philippines, and for good reason. This dish will blow your mind with its rich and complex flavors, while also being super easy to make!

Personally, I made this dish whenever I was in a cooking mood, as it does take a little more effort, but it is so good.

Serves 4-6 people. Cooking Time: ~1.5 hours + 2 hours prep

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp crushed black pepper
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 bay leaves

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, marinate the chicken thighs with soy sauce, 3 cloves of crushed garlic, oyster sauce, and crushed black pepper. Marinate for at least 1-2 hours, or overnight for best results.
  2. In a wok or saucepan, heat up oil on medium-high heat and sautee 3 cloves of minced garlic.
  3. Once fragrant, add the chicken thighs and sautee until they are browned.
  4. Add water and let the chicken simmer on low heat for around 40 minutes.
  5. Add vinegar, bay leaves. and sugar, and continue simmering the chicken for around 10 minutes.
  6. Serve with white rice!
